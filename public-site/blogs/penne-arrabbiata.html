Step-by-Step: Bringing Penne Arrabbiata to Life

  1. Start by heating 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add 3 cloves of sliced garlic and a teaspoon of red chili flakes, stirring until the garlic is golden and fragrant.
  2. Add a can of crushed tomatoes and let it simmer gently, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt to taste.
  3. Meanwhile, cook 300g of penne pasta according to package instructions, until al dente.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, drain and add it to the sauce, tossing to coat each piece in the spicy, tangy mixture.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley. If you like, top with freshly grated Parmesan for an extra layer of flavor.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I make Penne Arrabbiata less spicy?

    Absolutely! Simply reduce the amount of red chili flakes until it suits your spice tolerance.

  • What can I serve with Penne Arrabbiata?

    It pairs wonderfully with a fresh green salad or some crusty garlic bread to mop up the sauce.

  • Is Penne Arrabbiata vegan?

    Yes, the traditional recipe is vegan as it only uses plant-based ingredients. Just skip the Parmesan if avoiding dairy.
