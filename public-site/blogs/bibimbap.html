Frequently Asked Questions

What does Bibimbap mean?
Bibimbap translates to "mixed rice" in Korean, highlighting the central action of mixing all ingredients before eating.
Can Bibimbap be made vegetarian?
Absolutely! You can skip the meat and add tofu or extra vegetables for a delicious vegetarian version.
Is Bibimbap spicy?
Bibimbap can be as spicy as you prefer, depending on the amount of gochujang used. Adjust to your taste!
