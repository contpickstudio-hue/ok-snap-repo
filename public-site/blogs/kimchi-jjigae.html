FAQs

Can I make Kimchi Jjigae vegetarian?
Absolutely! Simply omit the pork and add more tofu or mushrooms for a hearty vegetarian version.
What type of kimchi is best for this stew?
Aged kimchi, sour and deeply fermented, works best as it provides a richer flavor to the stew.
How can I adjust the spiciness of the dish?
You can reduce the gochujang for a milder taste or add more if you prefer extra heat.
