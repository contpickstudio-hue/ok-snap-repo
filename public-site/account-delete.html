Account Deletion

Request to permanently delete your account and associated data

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your OK-Snap account, please contact us using one of the methods below. We'll process your request as soon as possible.

Contact Us Email Support

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Contact us via the contact form or email at support@ok-snap.com
  2. Include your account email address in your request
  3. Verify your identity if requested (for security purposes)
  4. We will confirm receipt of your deletion request
  5. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within the retention period

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Account Information: Your user account, email address, profile information, and authentication credentials
  • Scan History: All records of your food identification scans and related metadata
  • Images: All uploaded images and associated files stored in our system
  • Favorites: Your saved favorite recipes and dishes
  • Preferences: All user settings and preferences

Important Note

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have exported any data you wish to keep before requesting deletion.

Data Retention Period

Once your deletion request is received and verified, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. During this period:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • You will not be able to access your account or data
  • All data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help.
