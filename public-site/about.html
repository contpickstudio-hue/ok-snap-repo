About OK-Snap

Our Mission

OK-Snap is dedicated to sharing the rich flavors and cultural heritage of Korean cuisine with food lovers around the world. We combine modern technology with traditional recipes to help you discover, learn, and enjoy authentic Korean dishes.

What We Do

Using advanced AI technology, OK-Snap identifies Korean dishes from photos and provides detailed information including:

  • Authentic recipe instructions
  • Nutritional facts and calorie information
  • Cultural context and history
  • Cooking tips and techniques

Our Story

OK-Snap was born from a passion for Korean food and a desire to make it accessible to everyone. Whether you're a seasoned cook or just starting your culinary journey, we're here to guide you through the wonderful world of Korean cuisine.
