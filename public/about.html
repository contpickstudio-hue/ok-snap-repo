About Ok Snap

Ok Snap helps you discover food through your lens. Point your camera at a dish or upload a photo, and get instant recognition, recipe ideas, and cultural context—with a focus on Korean and global cuisines.

What We Do

  • Food recognition: Our AI identifies dishes from your photos and suggests matching recipes.
  • Recipes & stories: Explore full recipes and cultural background on our recipe site.
  • History & favorites: Sign in to save your scans and favorites across devices.
  • Multiple languages: Use the app in English, Korean, Spanish, French, and Chinese.

How It Works

Use the scanner at scanner.ok-snap.com to capture or upload a photo. Results link to recipe content on recipes.ok-snap.com. No app store download required—everything runs in your browser.
