Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Ok Snap ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our web app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: If you sign in (e.g., with email or Google), we store your email, display name, and profile picture as provided by the authentication provider. When you create an account, you confirm that you have read this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service and agree to how we handle your data.

Usage data: We may collect information about how you use the app, such as scan history, saved favorites, and preferences (e.g., language, theme) to provide and improve the service.

Photos you provide: Images you upload or capture are sent to our servers only for the purpose of food recognition and recipe suggestions. We do not use them for advertising or sell them to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide food recognition, recipe suggestions, and personalized content
  • Save your history and favorites across devices when you are signed in
  • Improve our services and fix issues
  • Send you important service-related notices if necessary

3. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to show ads on our web app. Google may collect and use data (such as cookies and device identifiers) to personalize ads and measure ad performance. You can learn more and control ad preferences at Google's Advertising Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase and other trusted providers to store your account and usage data. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage or delete your account and associated data from the app settings. For requests or questions, contact us using the details on our Contact page.

6. Children

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please see our Contact page.
